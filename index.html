Introduction

Time Magazine: “Guns in America"
325 million people. More than 265 million guns. 35,000 deaths a year. And one 227-year-old constitutional right.

The era of Trump will be remembered for the extreme polarization of America. With the constant fear of crime, gun deaths, and mass shootings, hostilities are at an all time high between those who want stronger gun legislation and those who believe their personal safety is based on their right to bear arms. Encouraged by the climate of fear mongering and nationalism by this administration, hate groups are emerging from the shadows and sowing further discord in America. The emotional power of Hate and the physical power of the gun are the two horns that threaten America!

Purpose

The purpose of this project was to provide interactive visualization of these polarizing issues. Are the varying gun laws effective at countering gun violence? Are hate groups embolden by the lack of gun laws they operate under? This analysis hopes to show how guns, laws, and hate are intertwined.
