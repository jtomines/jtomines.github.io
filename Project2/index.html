The Canadian Landscape for Data Science Careers

An ETL Project by The 6 in the Six

Title of article from Harvard Business Review: "Data Scientist: The Sexiest Job of the 21st Century." These are the types of slogans and buzz that are bombarding social and business media. This project seeks to uncover the Canadian landscape for those who are seeking careers in this emerging high-tech field. By analyzing data jobs being offered in websites like Indeed.com, and correlating them to the population and quality of life data where they are being offered, this project hopes to illuminate where the best opportunities and earning power can be found in Canada.

This group project, for the University of Toronto School of Continuing Studies DATA ANALYTICS Boot Camp, was to demonstrate the Extract-Transform-Load process. Using both API requests and direct downloads, data was extracted from Indeed (www.indeed.ca), Numbeo (www.numbeo.com), Statistics Canada (www.statcan.gc.ca) websites. Data was then cleaned, combined, and graphed using Python, before being uploaded to a MySQL database. The final deliverable for the project was a report describing the findings and the methodology used. The project team, nicknamed "The 6 in the Six", decided to go above and beyond by creating a web site as an alternative medium to the report. This webpage is a portal to view the report or the website.
