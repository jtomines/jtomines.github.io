My Library

My Love of Books

I have always loved books. As a child, the library/bookstore was my escape to new worlds or a deeper understanding of this one. History seen thru Dickens & Dumas, brave new worlds of Herbert, Asimov, & Tolkien, & social insights of Gladwell... could not get enough. Goju Ryu Karate

The Internal and External Struggle

As a teen inspired by martial arts films, I entered a dojo dreaming to become a lethal weapon... only to fall in love with tradition and philosophy of Goju Ryu Karate. I realized that the true adversary is yourself, physically and mentally. The preciseness required in kata, or tactical adaptability in kumite, facing your opponent is truly a battle against your own limitations!
