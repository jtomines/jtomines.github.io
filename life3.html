North Stars Basketball

Life as a Basketball Coach

With almost 30 years experience, I am a nationally certified coach (NCCP 1) who embraced a player-centric philosopy. Coaching from 6 year olds to mens division, 7-year championship streak, I've had players go NCAA and pro. This is my single-most important accomplishment outside of family, inspiring generations of youth with life lessons through basketball.

North Mississauga Soccer Club

Life as a Soccer Coach

With 22 years of experience, I am a nationally certified coach (NCCP 2) with OSA Adult level certification. A very successful house league, all-star, and select coach for North Mississauga Soccer Club, winning 4 league and 2 cup championships. Utilizing the same player-centric philosophy, I emphasized life lessons within my technical, tactical, and fitness drills.
